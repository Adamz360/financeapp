Excellence in Telecom our Firm's Expertise

Innovative ideas for fast-growing, digitally connected technology, media and telecoms companies keystone works with a wide range of clients in specialist areas from data logistics to international publishers to broadband technology providers, companies across the film, media and entertainment sector and telecommunications suppliers. Whatever your size and ambition, whether you are a growing business or a global multi-media company, we provide the same quality of service and commitment to each client - and our partner-led approach ensures a personal service. A selection of the services we provide to the Technology, media and telecommunications sectors are: Financial audit – with a current client portfolio including one of the largest publishers in the UK and the fourth largest communications agency in the world, we have the experience and expertise required to audit companies across the media sector. Our sector knowledge enables us to focus on key risk areas such as revenue recognition and the carrying value of brands, intangibles and goodwill. As such we are able to provide a tailored risk-based approach, maximising efficiency and adding value through the audit process.

Financial reporting advisory – our experienced team helps companies interpret and apply International Financial Reporting Standards and provides accounting advice on a range of projects. Particularly relevant for companies in the media sector, this includes accounting for business combinations and intangible asset valuations under IFRS3, and valuation of share option schemes, often used to incentivise employees in the creative environment. Licensing and royalty services - licensing of content is particularly prevalent in the media world, and sale of rights and licenses to companies around the world is a major driver of revenue for many media players. But how can companies be confident that the revenue reported from licensees is accurate? keystone provides help to media companies in this area by advising on their licensing strategy, performing royalty audits and carrying out investigative assignments where breaches have occurred. Our international reach means that we can perform these assignments on a global scale – leading to efficiency and a streamlined approach for our clients.

Tax planning and compliance - tax planning is particularly important in the rapidly changing media sector. keystone provides a full range of tax services for every stage of a company’s life cycle from advice on acquisitions, disposals and restructuring to international tax planning for global businesses. Our specialist employment tax team helps companies to reward and retain talent through tax-efficient remuneration planning, an area of high importance in the creative sector. Transaction services and corporate finance - in a dynamic and evolving sector, acquisitions, divestments, capital raising and restructuring are taking place every day. keystone helps media companies undertake these transactions by providing pre-deal due diligence, and post-deal transformation and acting as reporting accountants for IPOs and capital raising. Business performance improvement - in times of change, companies need to adapt their systems and processes quickly to seize opportunities and address new challenges effectively. Whether you are looking at ways to reduce costs in a challenging environment, or for improvements to your processes and management information, keystone can provide the expertise that you need.
